Computer Engineering

Computer engineering (CoE or CpE) is a branch of electrical engineering and computer science that integrates several fields of computer science and electronic engineering required to develop computer hardware and software. Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering is the study of physical machines that may involve force and movement.
